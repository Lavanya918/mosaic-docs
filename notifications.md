# Notifications

The **Notifications** panel in the MOSAIC platform provides real-time updates related to system activity, jobs, and user-specific events. It is accessible from the **bell icon** in the top-right header of the application.


## Accessing Notifications

- Click the **bell icon** in the top navigation bar.
- A drop-down panel opens displaying notifications.
- Notifications are grouped by category using three tabs/icons at the top of the panel.


## Notification Categories

The Notifications panel includes **three distinct notification types**, each accessible via an icon:

### 1. Tenant Notifications

**Purpose:**  
Tenant notifications are organization-level messages relevant to the user’s workspace.

**Examples:**
- Platform-wide announcements
- Service updates or maintenance notifications
- Policy or compliance-related messages
- Workspace or tenant configuration changes

**Visibility:**  
Visible to users associated with the tenant/workspace.


### 2. Job Notifications

**Purpose:**  
Job notifications track the progress and status of operational tasks and workflows within the platform.

**Examples:**
- Image upload completion
- Slide digitization status
- Image QC completion
- AI analysis start or completion
- Pathologist review completion
- Report generation status

**Usage Context:**  
These notifications help users monitor the lifecycle of orders and analysis jobs without navigating to individual order pages.

---

### 3. Viewer Notifications

**Purpose:**  
Viewer notifications are **user-specific alerts** related to actions or updates relevant to the logged-in user.

**Examples:**
- Orders submitted by the user
- Reports available for viewing
- Actions requiring user attention
- Access or permission-related updates

**Visibility:**  
Personalized and visible only to the logged-in user.

---

## Notification States

- If no notifications are available under a selected category, the panel displays:
  > **“No notifications”**
- Notifications may appear in chronological order, with the most recent updates shown first.
- Read and unread states may be visually differentiated (if enabled).

---

## Summary

The Notifications panel enables users to:
- Stay informed about **system-level updates**
- Track **job and workflow progress**
- Receive **personalized alerts** relevant to their actions and responsibilities

This centralized notification system improves visibility, responsiveness, and overall user experience within the MOSAIC platform.
